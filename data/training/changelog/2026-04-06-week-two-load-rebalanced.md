This week was rebalanced after the previous long run finished strongly and the earlier shin-pressure concern did not reappear during the session.

The Monday basketball session was removed because the public holiday cancels it. Rather than leave the day empty or replace it with another hard effort, Monday becomes a slightly longer `8 km` aerobic support run.

The rest of the week keeps Tuesday as the main quality session, but Sunday's long run moves from `14 km` to `15 km` so the block consolidates the distance already tolerated last weekend instead of stepping backward too sharply.

The downstream effect is a `63 km` planned week with one less basketball hit than originally scheduled. That keeps the progression real, but still ties the extra running volume to controlled aerobic work rather than another intensity spike.
