Revised Saturday `2026-06-13` after the athlete confirmed there will be no
run and no basketball tomorrow, with a hike planned instead.

The current week had already reached `50.111 km` from Tuesday through Friday
local time, including the hard `18.066 km` June 10 run and the June 12
`16.036 km` progression that replaced the intended recovery run. Removing the
Saturday `4 km` run and basketball session is therefore the cleaner
load-management choice.

The weekly table now lists Saturday as a hike-only cross-training day and
reduces the planned running total from `84 km` to `80 km`. The hike is recorded
as a mobility/cross-training note because the workout-note schema does not yet
have a dedicated hike event type.

The downstream coaching read is unchanged in principle but sharper in practice:
do not add Saturday running, do not replace basketball with another hard effort,
and keep Sunday's `22 km` long run conditional on how the legs feel after the
hike, especially if there is meaningful descending or long time on feet.
