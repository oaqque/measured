Added a plan analysis timeline entry for `2026-06-29` to `2026-07-05`, the
first major Sydney Marathon build week after the Parramatta Half Marathon.

The new entry records the week as `68.469 km` across five runs against the
planned `76 km`, with a `156.3 bpm` distance-weighted average HR and
`364.8 m` of Strava elevation gain. It separates the apparent distance deficit
from the actual load profile: Friday and Saturday easy volume was missed, but
Tuesday's `16.057 km` threshold run and Sunday's `22.367 km` progressive long
run made the week materially more intense than the raw shortfall suggests.

The downstream interpretation is that the week successfully reopened marathon
durability after Parramatta, but the next block needs cheaper easy running
rather than make-up kilometres. That keeps the Sydney `3:30:00` target focused
on repeatable marathon-specific volume instead of compressing too much quality
into Tuesday, Thursday, and Sunday.
