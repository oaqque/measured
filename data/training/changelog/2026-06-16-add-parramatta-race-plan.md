Expanded the planned Parramatta Half Marathon race note from a placeholder into
a full race-day execution plan.

The plan uses the official course page, runner guide, published course map, and
official Strava/Garmin route links to capture the practical course shape: an
O'Connell Street start, early CBD turns before 5 km, a middle rhythm section
through the river and Western Sydney University side, Parramatta Park around
15-20 km, and the CommBank Stadium finish. The runner guide's listed
`~140 m` elevation and aid stations at 5 km, 12 km, and 15 km are now reflected
in the plan.

The pacing guidance keeps `1:45:00` as the primary target because it matches the
current workout-supported VDOT range, while still treating `1:44:00-1:44:30` as
a good-day stretch rather than the default start-line assumption. The note also
adds heart-rate guardrails, fueling instructions, travel/bib logistics, and
contingencies for crowding, heavy legs, high early heart rate, or race-eve
basketball.
