The `2026-07-06` to `2026-07-12` week was rewritten after the reported calf tightness after the `2026-07-05` long run and next-day Achilles soreness.

The old `84 km` structure is no longer the right target. The revised week removes Tuesday threshold/HM-pace work, Thursday marathon-pace work, Saturday running, and normal basketball load. It becomes a `22 to 30 km` conditional return-to-run progression: three no-run recovery days, a `4 to 6 km` Thursday test, `6 to 8 km` Friday only if symptoms stay stable, and a capped `12 to 16 km` Sunday easy long run only if the tendon tolerates the earlier checks.

This deliberately protects the Sydney Marathon build by accepting one down week rather than trying to force a high-mileage block through an improving Achilles.
