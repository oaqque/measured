The plan now treats weekly gym work as a push, pull, and legs split rather than two generic gym sessions. The default structure is Tuesday legs, Thursday push, and Saturday pull, with light variants during Parramatta and Sydney Marathon race weeks.

The change keeps strength training explicit while narrowing each session's job: legs build lower-body force and basketball durability, push maintains pressing strength and shoulder health, and pull supports upper-back strength, trunk control, grip, and contact resilience. The gym notes from `2026-05-12` through the Sydney Marathon taper were replaced to match those roles.

The downstream coaching impact is that strength work should maintain muscle mass and support basketball without competing with the key running sessions. If fatigue accumulates, gym load should be reduced before the Tuesday quality run, Thursday quality run, or Sunday long run are compromised.
