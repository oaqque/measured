The plan now assumes basketball on Monday nights and Saturday mornings for the remaining build. Those sessions are treated as hard court load rather than background activity, with race-eve Saturday basketball marked as something to skip if possible.

The weekly strength split moved from Tuesday legs, Thursday push, and Saturday pull to Wednesday legs, Thursday push, and Friday pull. This keeps heavy lower-body lifting away from Tuesday and Thursday quality runs, Sunday long runs, and the Monday/Saturday basketball slots.

Future strength and basketball notes were regenerated to match the new placement. Wednesday legs are written as heavy-low-volume by default, with an explicit instruction to reduce to light technique work if Monday basketball or Tuesday quality running leaves the legs flat.
