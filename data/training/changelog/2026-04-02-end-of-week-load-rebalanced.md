The remainder of the week was adjusted after the threshold session shifted back a day and Saturday basketball was removed.

The reshaped block now does three things:

- Thursday backs off into easy aerobic work rather than stacking another moderate run straight after threshold.
- Friday and Saturday keep the legs moving without adding more load than needed.
- Sunday becomes a more useful easy long run, using the space created by dropping basketball.
