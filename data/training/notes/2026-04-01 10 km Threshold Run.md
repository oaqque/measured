## Program

- 2 km easy warm-up
- 3 x 8 minutes at threshold effort with 2 minutes easy jog between reps
- Cool down easy to 10 km total

## Session Structure

- Start the first 10 to 15 minutes genuinely easy, then add 3 to 4 short relaxed strides before the first threshold rep.
- Run the threshold work at a controlled pace you could hold for roughly 45 to 60 minutes of racing, not 5 km effort. It should feel strong and steady rather than ragged.
- Keep the recoveries as easy jog, not standing rest, so the session stays aerobic and honest.
- If the legs still feel flat after Monday's easy run plus basketball, keep the same structure but slightly back off the pace and prioritize even execution across all three reps.

## Targets

- Threshold reps should feel around `7/10` effort.
- The third rep should still look similar to the first.
- If form or breathing starts to drift badly before the final rep, you are running too hard.
