- Keep the whole run conversational and smooth. This is not a second quality day.
- Start the first 10 minutes very controlled, then settle into relaxed aerobic running.
- If the legs still feel loaded from the delayed threshold session on `2026-04-01`, shorten slightly rather than forcing the full distance at the wrong effort.

## Targets

- Effort should stay around `4 to 5/10`.
- Heart rate should sit clearly below threshold-session effort.
- Finish feeling better than you did at the halfway point.

## Rationale

- The original week had steady work on Thursday, but the threshold session slipped from Tuesday to Wednesday.
- That makes back-to-back quality too aggressive for this stage of the build.
- The priority for the rest of the week is to absorb Wednesday's work, keep frequency, and still arrive at Sunday with enough freshness for a useful long run.
