## Program

- 15 km easy long run
- Keep the first 4 to 5 km deliberately patient, then settle into smooth aerobic running without forcing the pace.
- The goal is to consolidate the distance you handled well last weekend, not to turn the run into a progression test.
- If the shins stay quiet, finish the final 2 km by holding form and rhythm rather than speeding up.
- If shin pressure returns or Thursday/Saturday load lingers, cap the run at `13 to 14 km` instead of forcing the full distance.
