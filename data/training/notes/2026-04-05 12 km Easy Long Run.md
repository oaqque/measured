## Program

- 12 km easy long run

## Session Structure

- Keep the first third patient and controlled.
- Settle into steady aerobic running through the middle of the run without pushing the pace.
- Finish the final 2 to 3 km by staying smooth and upright, not by forcing a fast close.

## Targets

- Effort should stay around `4 to 5/10` for most of the run.
- The run should feel like endurance work, not a disguised progression run.
- If fatigue is still unusually high from the delayed threshold session, cap the run at `10 to 11 km` rather than forcing `12`.

## Rationale

- Saturday no longer includes basketball, so the weekend can support a slightly more useful long run.
- Moving the threshold session to Wednesday still means the main job here is aerobic durability, not heroics.
- The best outcome is finishing this week with three things intact: frequency, one real quality session, and a calm long run.
